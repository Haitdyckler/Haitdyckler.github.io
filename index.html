Arduino Alarm Clock

For My IoT project, I made an Arduino Alarm clock, that ran from an esp32. The circuit was equipped with humidity sensors, 4 buttons to set the time for the alarm, an lcd screen, speaker and a motion sensor.

When equipped with more functions with a Wi-Fi, I was able to connect the board to the internet and make the clock run on normal where it is, an you read would be a good supply to power for at least a week.
